### Christiaan de Ridder

I am a marketer who builds. 15 years in B2B, ex-AWS EMEA,
now Head of Global Marketing at Nordcloud, an IBM company.

I build CoNudge, a SaaS for personal trainers. The AI writes
every line of code. I have not read one of them. My job is to
decide what ships and what dies. That judgment is the part the
machine does not have. It is what I brought from marketing.

For a while I thought the skill I lacked was thinking like a
developer. I was wrong. The AI already thinks like a developer.
It optimizes for code that is defensible, not for what the user
gets. I drag every decision back to the person using it.

How I work: I trust no single source, including the AI. Three
models in parallel, mechanical checks as the judge, instinct as
the tiebreaker. A model that lies politely is the most expensive
tool you own.

Most of what I build stays private. This profile shows the work
is real, not to give the code away.

I write about building and deciding with AI, and about selling to
business in EMEA: christiaanderidder.com
